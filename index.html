We at STRAWIDEA aim at changing habits and mindsets about using disposable items. We're committed to creating products and services that inspire and support people to make positive lifestyle changes, consume more sustainably, and live better everyday lives. To meet the challenges of unsustainable consumption, climate change, and growing inequality, we are-in collaboration with rural India, co-workers, and customers-taking ambitious steps towards a more sustainable future.
Eco-friendly

Our products are made from Areca leaf which are 100%. biodegradable and compostable. These are hygienic, non-odorous, nontoxic, light weight plates.

Say NO to plastic.

A perfect alternative to plastic plates and cutlery our products is eco-friendly as they do not have behind toxic chemical or residues.

Save Earth

All our products will biodegrade naturally in the soil in 60-90 days in the presence of microbes. Works just as well for landfills too.

Energy Efficient

Biodegradable plates & compostable disposables are more energy effecient compared to manufacturing single-us-plastics.

100% Vegan

Areca leaf products are 100% vegan cruelty free, no chemicals or additives used in the entire manufacturing process. our products are manufactured from fallen dry leaves of the tree.

Famer Employment

About three million farmers are dependent on areca nut for their livelihood as it generates enormous employment and income.

Microwave Safe

Can be safely used in a microwave, heater, or oven and sustain temperatures from -20° C to +140° C

No Mess No Stress

Lets you focus on the food by providing a hassle-free serving option. Disposal is quick and easy because no waste segregation is required at the source.
